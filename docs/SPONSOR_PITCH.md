# Ajora Sponsor Pitch & Target List

## Target List

Focus on brands operating in regions where mobile-first engagement is critical (e.g., Nigeria, Kenya, Ghana). Ideal profiles:
1. **Telecoms**: MTN, Airtel, Safaricom. They already spend heavily on SMS marketing.
2. **Fintech / Neobanks**: OPay, Moniepoint, Chipper Cash. They are aggressively trying to acquire engaged retail users.
3. **FMCG**: Coca-Cola Africa, Promasidor. High volume, daily touchpoints.

## The Pitch

**Subject**: Reach 10,000+ daily active users for $0.01 CAC.

**Body**:
Hi [Name],

- **Unbeatable CAC**: Sponsoring a $50/day pot exposes your brand to our entire active user base (often 5,000+ DAU), translating to an effective engagement cost of less than $0.01 per user.
